Attorney Tamara Evelyn

Attorney Tamara Evelyn is a proud graduate of Massachusetts School of Law. Upon graduation, she served as a Bristol County Assistant District Attorney practicing in both Juvenile Court and District Court . She established her solo practice in 2016 with a primary focus of family law, juvenile law and civil litigation.

She is a member of Massachusetts Bar Association and Plymouth County Bar Association. In May of 2022, she was awarded the Emerging Defender Award for her commitment to indigent defense.
